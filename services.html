Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

Typical website projects take 4-6 weeks from start to finish, depending on the complexity and scope. Simple landing pages may be completed in as little as 2 weeks, while complex e-commerce sites might take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. These include regular backups, security monitoring, WordPress updates, and technical support when needed.

How do you measure the success of social media campaigns?

We track key performance indicators like engagement rate, follower growth, reach, impressions, click-through rates, and conversions. We provide detailed monthly reports showing these metrics and how they translate to your business goals.

Can you help with content creation?

Absolutely! We offer copywriting, photography, and graphic design services to create compelling content for your website and social media channels. We can work with existing brand guidelines or help develop a new content strategy from scratch.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Yes, we work with clients nationwide and internationally. Our collaborative tools allow us to communicate effectively regardless of location, and we're always available for video calls and remote meetings.
